PROFILE

Traveling Tricycle Travel Blog: The idea here was to create a cool platform to share my recent adventures in Southeast Asia. I decided that my primary focus would be on the pictues, and using all available screen space to display them. I put together a completely reactive site with a customizable grid system to accomplish this, all on a tiny laptop while backpacking (blech, trackpads). The finished product also features a picture based main navigation page with parallax scroll, and a cute little Javascript/CSS widget that allows you to change the background color of individual posts.
